Recent Project & Achievements

NAiV : Indoor Navigation App

NAiV is an ongoing project on indoor navigation app, ideated and being developed by Team Hexdev(previously known as Technocrats) that I am proud to be part of. NAiV deals with navigation within buildings as GPS reception is non-existent inside closed space. The app scans a QRcode and extracts a digital image of the building from its database. Positioning technologies like trilateration of Wifi signals & RSSI fingerprinting determines the exact position & floor level of the user. A* Algorithms(AI) then generates the shortest path from the user's location to the destination. This project was one of the top 7 teams from NITW to qualify for the Smart India Hackathon 2020. Team Hexdev was lauded as we were the only 1st year students to achieve this feat.
